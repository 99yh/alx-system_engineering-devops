## Regular Expressions
How to make genaric searching patterns

- literal character: anything that is not meta characater
- meta character: . * [] () ?
    -single char:  \d \w \s
    -quantifiers: . ? * + {howmany} {min,max}
    -positioners: ^ $ \b
- char classes: [abc] [a-c] [^d-z]
- alternation: (cat|dog|elephant)
